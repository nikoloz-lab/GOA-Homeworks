Simple Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the beaten eggs.
  4. As the eggs begin to set, use a spatula to gently push the cooked edges toward the center, tilting the pan to allow uncooked egg to flow to the edges.
  5. When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly wet on top, add your chosen fillings to one half of the omelette.
  6. Using the spatula, carefully fold the empty half over the fillings.
  7. Cook for another minute until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully set.
